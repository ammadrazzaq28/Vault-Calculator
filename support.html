Frequently asked questions

How do I open my vault?

Open Calculator Vault and use it like a normal calculator. Enter your secret PIN followed by the equals (=) key to unlock your private vault. If you set up Face ID or Touch ID, you can also use biometrics after entering your PIN once.

What is the decoy vault?

If you enable a decoy PIN in Settings, entering that PIN opens a separate, harmless vault instead of your real one. This helps protect your secrets if someone forces you to unlock the app.

How do I import photos or videos?

After unlocking your vault, go to Photos or Videos and tap the import button. Grant Photo Library access when prompted. Imported items are stored encrypted in your vault on your device.

Why can't Calculator Vault access my photos or camera?

Go to Settings → Calculator Vault on your iPhone and enable Photos and Camera permissions. If you previously denied access, turn them on there and relaunch the App.

Is my data uploaded to your servers?

Your vault contents are encrypted and stored on your iPhone. We do not operate a cloud copy of your photos, videos, notes, or passwords. See our Privacy Policy for analytics and advertising details.

I forgot my PIN. Can you reset it?

For your security, we cannot recover or reset your vault PIN. If you forget your PIN, vault data cannot be accessed. We recommend using Face ID or Touch ID as a backup unlock method.

How do I cancel Calculator Vault Premium?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. On your iPhone: Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions → Calculator Vault → Cancel Subscription. Cancel at least 24 hours before renewal to avoid the next charge.

How do I restore my purchase?

On the subscription screen in the App, tap Restore. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

What does the 3-day free trial include?

Eligible new subscribers receive premium access for three days, including ad-free use and unlimited vault storage. If you do not cancel before the trial ends, Apple automatically bills the subscription shown on the paywall.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds are processed by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com and sign in with your Apple ID.

Still need help?
