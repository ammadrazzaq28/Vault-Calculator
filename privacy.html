Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026 · App: Calculator Vault (com.ammad.vault.calculator.secret)

1. Introduction

Calculator Vault ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use the App, how we use it, and the choices you have.

By downloading or using Calculator Vault, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

2. Who we are

3. Information we collect

3.1 Information processed on your device

Core vault features process data on your iPhone. When you use Calculator Vault, the App may access:

  • Photos & videos — to import, store, view, and optionally export or delete items you add to your vault
  • Camera — to capture intruder selfie photos when that optional security feature is enabled and an incorrect PIN is entered
  • Vault contents — encrypted photos, videos, notes, and passwords you choose to store
  • PIN & security settings — vault PIN hashes, decoy PIN, biometric preferences, and intruder alert settings stored locally

We do not require you to create an account. We do not ask for your name, email, or phone number to use core App features.

3.2 Information stored on your device

Vault data, settings, and app preferences are stored locally on your iPhone using encrypted storage and the device Keychain where appropriate. We do not operate our own cloud database for your vault contents unless we explicitly tell you otherwise in a future update.

3.3 Device permissions

Calculator Vault may request access to:

  • Photo Library — to import and save photos and videos in your vault
  • Camera — for optional intruder selfie capture after failed unlock attempts
  • Face ID / Touch ID — for optional biometric vault unlock

You can revoke these permissions at any time in iPhone Settings → Calculator Vault.

3.4 Purchases & subscriptions

If you subscribe to Calculator Vault Premium, payment is processed by Apple. We receive subscription status and related receipt information needed to unlock premium features. We do not receive or store your full payment card number.

3.5 Analytics, configuration & advertising

We may use third-party services that collect limited technical or usage information, such as:

  • Firebase Analytics & Remote Config — aggregated usage events and remote feature settings to improve the App
  • Google AdMob — if you are not subscribed, the App may show ads; AdMob may collect device and ad interaction data according to Google's policies
  • RevenueCat — subscription status and purchase validation
  • Device type, operating system version, and App version
  • Crash or performance data if you opt in to sharing diagnostics with developers via Apple

3.6 App Tracking Transparency

On supported iOS versions, we may ask for permission to track your device across other companies' apps and websites for advertising measurement. If you deny permission, we respect your choice and do not access the advertising identifier (IDFA) for that purpose.

4. How we use information

We use information to:

  • Provide vault storage, encryption, calculator disguise, and security features
  • Validate and manage Calculator Vault Premium subscriptions
  • Display ads to non-subscribers and measure ad performance when permitted
  • Improve reliability, security, and user experience through analytics and remote configuration
  • Comply with legal obligations and respond to support requests

We do not sell your personal information.

5. Storage & retention

Vault data and preferences remain on your device until you delete the App or clear relevant App data. Subscription records are retained as long as needed to provide the service and meet legal or accounting requirements.

If you contact support, we retain correspondence for a reasonable period to resolve your request.

6. Sharing & third parties

We may share limited information with:

  • Apple Inc. — App Store purchases and system services
  • Google (Firebase, AdMob) — analytics, remote configuration, and advertising as described above
  • RevenueCat — subscription management
  • Legal authorities — when required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security

We do not upload your vault photos, videos, notes, or passwords to our own servers for advertising purposes.

7. Vault data & security

Vault contents are encrypted on your device. Your PIN is not stored in plain text. Processing happens on your iPhone unless a feature clearly states otherwise. We do not use your vault contents for unrelated advertising profiles.

If you forget your PIN, vault data cannot be recovered by us — this is by design to protect your privacy. Always keep your PIN safe and consider enabling biometric unlock as a backup.

8. App tracking & ads

You can control tracking in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking on your iPhone. Subscribers may see fewer or no ads depending on App configuration. Denying tracking does not block free features that do not require a subscription, subject to feature limits shown in the App.

9. Your rights & choices

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete personal information we hold about you
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Withdraw consent (for example, photo, camera, or tracking permissions) at any time via device settings
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority (EEA/UK users)

Because most vault data is processed on your device, you can remove it by deleting the App or revoking permissions. For subscription management, use Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions on your iPhone.

California residents may have additional rights under the CCPA/CPRA. We do not sell personal information as defined by California law.

10. Children

Calculator Vault is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

11. International users

If you use the App outside your home country, information may be processed on your device and by Apple or service providers (such as Google and RevenueCat) in accordance with their terms. By using the App, you understand that those providers may process data in countries where they operate.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the revised version on this page and update the effective date. Material changes may also be communicated in the App where appropriate.
